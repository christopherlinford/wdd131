Site Name

Christopher Linford Audio – This name highlights the brand of my brother's professional audio engineering service. It’s direct and easy to remember, which supports visibility and branding. It also ties his name to his profession.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to market my brother’s audio engineering services. It will showcase his talent, provide information on services, feature client testimonials, and include tools for contacting or booking him. The goal is to help him grow his client base and display his skills online.

Scenarios

  • What are the mixing and mastering prices for different types of audio projects?
  • How can I book a session at the recording studio with Christopher?
